Personnal experience

Following the pandemic and the changes it brought, I decided to leave my job to try my hand at entrepreneurship. It was a very rewarding experience, both personally and professionally, and it lasted two years. Faced with the difficulty of earning a decent salary, I decided to embark on a professional reorientation which led me to code this website.
